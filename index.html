What is Scinnova?

From a participant’s standpoint, Scinnova will be an experience to remember. Taking ideas out of the classroom and putting them in front of you. Create complex circuitry. Dissect creatures with paper-thin precision. Unravel conspiracies. In Scinnova, rediscover yourself, go outside your comfort zone and get a firsthand experience of how it feels to work in the practical world.

The word "Scinnova" stems from the words "Science" and "Innovation." The vision behind this is to challenge young minds in General Sciences, Programming and Robotics, Psychology, and much more. Scinnova is more than just a Science Olympiad; it ignites the flames of passion for science in an individual. It lets you explore yourself and venture STEM beyond theory and connects you with the essence of these domains.

How are we different?

Since its inception, practical-based learning has been instilled deep into Scinnova's roots. While others are still using conventional methods for their Olympiads, we develop innovative methods that force the participants to "Think Outside The Box". At Scinnova, we are driven towards providing a well-rounded experience that fuels the creative mind and captures the true essence of STEM-based learning.

The Brains Behind Scinnova

Scinnova, Cedar College's flaghsip Science Olympiad, is made possible by the following prestigious STEM societies

CoRDS

CoRDS, The Council of Research and Development in Sciences aims to make science more accessible to its student body.We strive to eliminate the gap between textbook-based learning and the practical implementation of ideas in Science.

CREW

With a goal to automate anything and everything possible, Cedar's Robotics and Engineering Wing, also known as CREW, aims to bring innovations and offer its technical expertise to make a more efficient Scinnova and enhance the overall Cedar experience.

Juno

The Juno Circle, a blend of astrophysics and creative and artistic energies, focuses on the cosmos and its eloquence. This astronomy and astrophysics society has been an integral part of Scinnova since its second edition and continues to contribute.
