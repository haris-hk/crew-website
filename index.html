What is CREW?

CREW is a diverse stem society that provides one with insights and exposure in multiple different areas such as those listed below

Coding

Programming is an integral part of CREW as it overlaps with various other aspects of CREW, including robotics and web development. We are also reputed for organizing various coding workshops and competitions

Engineering

The engineer in each member of CREW is what drives us to undertake the most complex of projects. From making Solar powered charging stations to installing automatic water tap systems in the campuses

Robotics

CREW is almost synonymous with this complex field of science, members of CREW have achieved a lot through robotics such as building line following robots, blind stick and there is so much more we plan to do in future

What do we offer?

We at CREW not only offer you a place to learn and experience the wonderful world of robotics and engineering, but also give you a work environment that helps you develop skills which will help you greatly throughout your professional life

CREATIVE FREEDOM -At CREW we work as a team. With every given task, you have the opportunity to take it to the best level you can and there are no restrictions to what you are willing to do; there is only constructive feedback and valuable opinions which will aide you in doing the best you can and also help you learn a thing or two from the experienced members of CREW

Workshops

CREW puts its efforts into conducting informative and intellectual workshops. These workshops incorporate lectures on advancements in technology and how students can fashion them on their own, followed by construction activities that help them to produce problem-solving, creative thinking, and decision-making skills. CREW, in 2021, successfully hosted the mesmerizing Inventor’s Binary Workshop, a hands-on, beginner-friendly introduction to the art of Arduino and robotics. It was an interactive session where the students and the team in conjunction learned about Arduino, coding, and writing algorithms that work alongside a robot, sensors, motors, and much more.
